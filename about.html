About Us

Our Story Connecting Talent, Powering Success

Teknora was founded with a clear vision: to bridge the gap between talented professionals and businesses in need of specialized expertise. What began as a small team with a passion for connecting people has grown into a trusted partner for companies across multiple industries. Our journey started with a focus on personalized recruitment solutions, and over time, we expanded to offer comprehensive HR services, from talent acquisition to onboarding and employee development.

Why Teknora

Where Technology Meets Purpose-Driven Hiring

At Teknora, we believe recruitment is more than a transaction it's a transformation. Founded and led by women, our firm stands at the intersection of technology, inclusion, and industry expertise. We combine the power of human intuition with the precision of artificial intelligence to help businesses hire smarter, faster, and more meaningfully.

We’re not just here to submit resumes we’re here to shape workforces that thrive.

Women-Led Business

As a women-led business, diversity isn’t just a goal it’s in our DNA. We’re committed to building inclusive teams and empowering organizations to create equitable work environments. This foundation allows us to approach recruitment with empathy, perspective, and a deep commitment to representation across all industries

Tailored Hiring, Not Templates

Every organization is unique and so is every hire. That’s why we don’t rely on cookie-cutter processes. We work closely with your internal teams to understand your business, culture, and hiring goals. Acting as a true partner, we build custom recruitment strategies that align with your growth — whether you need one specialist or an entire department.

Domain Expertise You Can Trust

From tech startups to EPC giants, we’ve helped companies across sectors like IT, Manufacturing, Oil & Gas, Construction, Retail, and more build top-tier teams. Our recruiters don’t just screen candidates — they understand the industry language, challenges, and skill requirements that make for a great hire in your domain.

Faster Time-to-Hire with Vizh AI

Our proprietary AI screening tool, Vizh AI, accelerates the hiring cycle without compromising quality. It conducts smart assessments, generates relevant interview questions, and produces detailed candidate reports giving you a clear, data-backed view of every applicant. The result? A hiring process that’s up to 50% faster and 10x more precise.

Flexible, Value-Driven Pricing

We know hiring budgets aren’t one-size-fits-all. Our pricing is built around value, not volume — driven by performance, not process. Thanks to our AI-powered workflow, we can reduce costs and pass those savings on to you, offering enterprise-level service with startup-friendly flexibility.

More Than a Vendor. A True Talent Partner.

We don’t just work for you we work with you. Our team becomes an extension of yours, driven by shared success, clear communication, and a relentless commitment to finding the right fit. That’s the Teknora difference.
